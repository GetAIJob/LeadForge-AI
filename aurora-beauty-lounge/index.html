Our Work

Results speak clearly

Our Studio

Built on one principle: your skin comes first.

I trained in Paris and spent four years working in larger salons before opening Aurora in 2016. I loved the craft, but I kept watching clients get shuffled through back-to-back appointments with products chosen off a promotional shelf, not off what their skin actually needed. So I kept the studio small on purpose — four clients a day, maximum — because that's the only way I can do the work properly.

Every client who walks through our door gets a consultation card on their first visit, and that card stays on file. Before I touch your skin, I want to know what you're dealing with — your routine, your stress levels, any sensitivities, what's changed since we last spoke. I choose products per person, not per brand agreement. If something isn't working for you, I'll say so.

We're not trying to be the biggest studio in Florence. We're trying to be the one you recommend to your closest friend — because you trust it that much. Eight years in, the fact that 97% of our clients come back tells us we're doing something right.

  • Maximum 4 clients per day — your appointment gets full attention
  • Products selected per client, not per brand deal
  • Your consultation card stays on file — every visit builds on the last
